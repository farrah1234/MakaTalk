• Welcome to MakaTalk


    MakaTalk is an iPad app that uses Makaton sign language to help young people with learning difficulties to express their thoughts and feelings more clearly before and after a therapy session.

    It is intended to replace the existing paper forms that patients are expected to fill out. These forms tend to capture less accurate data because they are often filled out by a parent or guardian instead of the young person. MakaTalk is more engaging and tactile.

    There are two touch screen surveys to choose from.

    The pre-session survey is for assessing a young person's mental and emotional wellbeing before they go into a therapy session. It asks how they are feeling at home and school, for example.

    The post-session survey is for checking what the young person thought about their therapy session. It asks if they felt understood by the clinician, for example.

    The answers are stored temporarily and without a patient name to protect anonymity. The clinician can reference this anonymous data immediately after the survey has been completed. They also have the option to send a survey report to their own email address. When MakaTalk is closed, current patient data will be deleted.
